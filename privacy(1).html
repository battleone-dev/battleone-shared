Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following information:

  • Personal details such as name, email address, and phone number

  • Payment data, processed securely via third-party gateways
  • Device data and platform usage information

2. Purpose of Data Collection

Your information is used to:

  • Operate, manage, and improve our services
  • Process payments and communicate transaction updates
  • Validate user identity and gaming credentials
  • Share platform updates, announcements, and promotions
  • Identify and prevent fraudulent or abusive activities

3. Data Protection Measures

We employ industry-accepted safeguards such as encryption, secured servers, and routine security checks to protect user data. Despite our efforts, absolute security over the internet cannot be guaranteed.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only with:

  • Payment partners required to complete transactions
  • Government or legal authorities when mandated by law
  • Trusted service providers supporting platform operations

5. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • View and access their personal information
  • Request updates or corrections to incorrect data
  • Request deletion of their account
  • Unsubscribe from promotional communications

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

BattleOne uses cookies and similar tools to enhance user experience, analyze platform usage, and personalize content. Cookie preferences can be managed through your browser settings.
